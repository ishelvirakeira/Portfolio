Intro

From Numbers to Code: My Journey

For me, it starts with a love of numbers and problem-solving. With a background in mathematics, digital and computational studies, and French, I've always been drawn to patterns, whether in data, language, or design.

I enjoy taking on community-engaged projects where technology can make a real difference. From simplifying processes to building tools that bring people together, I've seen how even small improvements can create a meaningful impact.

Now, I channel that passion into web development. Each project is a chance to learn, grow, and build something that not only works, but also makes life a little better for those who use it.
